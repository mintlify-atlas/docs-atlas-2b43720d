---
title: "Family protection DNS providers for safe browsing"
description: "DNS providers that block adult content, malware, and phishing — suitable for households with children or shared devices requiring content controls."
---

Family protection providers block adult and explicit content in addition to malware and phishing, making them appropriate for shared devices or households with children. Because filtering happens at the DNS level, it applies to all apps on the device without requiring any additional software. You can combine these profiles with other parental controls for a layered approach.

<Warning>
  DNS-based content filtering can be bypassed by switching to a different DNS resolver or by using a VPN. For stronger enforcement on managed devices, pair these profiles with device management policies that prevent profile removal.
</Warning>
